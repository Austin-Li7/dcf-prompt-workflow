## Step 1 validation scope

### Reportable segments
Adobe FY2025 10-K 披露 FY2025 的三个 reportable segments 为：
- **Digital Media**
- **Digital Experience**
- **Publishing and Advertising**

### Core metrics validated
- **FY2025 Total revenue** = **$23,769M**
- **FY2025 Digital Media revenue** = **$17,649M**
- **FY2025 Digital Experience revenue** = **$5,864M**
- **FY2025 Publishing and Advertising revenue** = **$256M**
- **FY2025 ending Digital Media ARR** = **$19,200M**

## 3. Porter’s Five Forces Framework
For **EACH Product Category**, provide a structured 5-force analysis. Rate each force as **Low, Medium, or High** and provide a 1-2 sentence strategic justification:
1.  **Intensity of Rivalry:** Number of competitors, industry growth rate, exit barriers.
2.  **Threat of New Entrants:** Barriers to entry, capital requirements, economies of scale, brand loyalty.
3.  **Bargaining Power of Suppliers:** Concentration of inputs/talent, switching costs for the firm, threat of forward integration.
4.  **Bargaining Power of Buyers:** Customer concentration, price sensitivity, information symmetry.
5.  **Threat of Substitute Products:** Alternative technologies or methods outside the direct industry that satisfy the same need.
